What you need to start climbing!

Rock climbing is a great activity and a fun way to stay inshape and get outside. Below you will find the basics you need to get started as well as some additional gear to enhance your experience.

Absolute esentials

In numerical order we have listed what you need to get started as soon as possible

  1. Climbing Shoes
  2. Harness
  3. Chalk

Thats it! it could be argued that all you really need is climbing shoes and in reality you could just borrow a friends chalk bag but ideally you would get these three things for the most enjoyable experience

Less than essential but still important

  • Dynamic rope
  • Locking carabiners
  • Belay device
  • Anchor building materials i.e. slings, cordelete
  • Quick clips
  • Cams/stoppers
  • helmet

You might be thinking, "Isnt a rope essential?" and you would be right. However it is your first time going you should be going with someone who already knows what they are doing and you can just use theirs!
